# IFS Research Taxonomy

**Purpose:**  
This taxonomy defines the controlled vocabulary used to catalog, annotate, and synthesize documents in the IFS Research Archive.

Its goal is to ensure consistency, clarity, and intellectual discipline when describing themes, methods, and implications across the research portfolio.

This taxonomy is intentionally limited. New terms should be added sparingly and only when they clarify, not multiply, categories.

---

## 1. Document Type

Each PDF should be assigned **one primary document type**.

- **Research Report**  
  Original empirical analysis, often with methodology and findings sections.

- **Policy Brief**  
  Short-form analysis translating research into policy-relevant insights.

- **Index / Dashboard**  
  Composite indicators or comparative tools (state, metro, national).

- **Survey / Polling Report**  
  Public opinion or demographic survey results.

- **Literature Review**  
  Synthesis of existing academic or policy literature.

- **Legislative / Policy Summary**  
  Summaries of bills, legislative proposals, or regulatory frameworks.

- **Letter / Statement**  
  Public-facing communications (letters, sign-ons, statements).
